# About NeedMCP

NeedMCP was created to solve one of the most persistent challenges in AI-assisted development: **the lack of consistent, high-quality design context for AI coding models.**

While modern AI assistants are exceptionally good at writing code, they often struggle with design consistency. When asked to "build a modern card component," different AI models—or even the same model in different sessions—will interpret "modern" in widely different ways. This leads to fragmented UIs, design debt, and excessive manual cleanup.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to empower developers to build professional, visually stunning interfaces using AI without sacrificing design integrity. We provide the "design brains" for your AI assistant.

## How It Works

NeedMCP utilizes the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**, an open standard that allows AI assistants to securely and efficiently interact with external data sources and tools.

By connecting your assistant to NeedMCP, you provide it with:
- **Pre-vetted Components**: Production-ready code snippets using Design Token.
- **Design Tokens**: Precise information about spacing, typography, and color palettes.
- **Semantic Understanding**: The AI understands not just *how* a component looks, but *why* it fits within a specific design system.

## Key Principles

- **Framework Agnostic**: We provide the logic and structure. Your AI adapts it to React, Vue, Svelte, Flutter, or any framework you choose.
- **Design Excellence**: Every style in our library is curated by professional designers to ensure accessibility, responsiveness, and modern aesthetics.
- **Developer First**: We believe AI should be a partner, not a replacement. NeedMCP streamlines the tedious parts of UI construction so you can focus on building features.

## The Team

NeedMCP is built by a team of developers and designers who are passionate about the intersection of AI and frontend engineering. We are constantly expanding our library of styles and improving our tools to better serve the developer community.
