## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

When you register and use our service, we collect:

- **Email address** - for account management and communication
- **API Key** - for authentication and API access
- **Account information** - provided during registration

### 2.2 Usage Logs

We automatically log API usage which includes:

- **User ID** - your account identifier
- **Action** - type of action performed (e.g., fetch_component, fetch_design_system)
- **Context** - additional details such as style slug and component types
- **Timestamp** - when the action was performed

### 2.3 Feedback

When you use the `sendFeedback` feature:

- **Feedback content** - the text you submit
- **Style slug** - the theme associated with the feedback
- **Feedback type** - category (ineffective_component, missing_component, suggestion)

### 2.4 Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Session cookies** - to maintain your login and session information
- These cookies are essential for the service to function properly
